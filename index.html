About Us

RETINA SECURITY SERVICES

Retina Security and Outsourcing Services Private Limited is a well-established and renowned security and manpower services which operates Bhubaneswar, Odisha-751030. The company delivers ISO 9001:2015 certified services and holds all requisite licenses mandated by the Government of India under the PSARA Act which include EPF and ESIC. Our Company was established on 08 Mar 2022 under the sole Directorship of Subedar Major(Retd) Benudhar Senapati and Narmada Senapaty. Subedar Major(Retd) Benudhar Senapati, Managing Director, Junior Commissioned Officer of Indian Army with distinguished service having achieved many honours and awards during service period and had served for the country in various operations Like Operation Parakram and LOC Kargil.

Customized Solutions

We believe that one size doesn't fit all when it comes to security. We work closely with each client to tailor our services to their specific needs and requirements. Our personalized approach ensures that you receive the level of security that is right for you.

  • Comprehensive Risk Assessment: We begin by conducting a thorough assessment of your unique security needs. Our experts analyze your property, assets, operations, and potential vulnerabilities to develop a detailed understanding of your specific security challenges.
  • Personalized Security Plans: Based on the assessment, we create a customized security plan that addresses your specific concerns and requirements.
  • Flexibility and Adaptability: Our approach is adaptable to changes and evolving threats. We understand that security needs can change over time, and we are ready to adjust our strategies to accommodate new challenges or circumstances..
  • Event-Specific Security: For events or temporary situations, we design security plans that cater to the unique needs of the occasion. Whether it's a corporate gathering, concert, or trade show, we ensure that your event runs smoothly and securely.
  • Emergency Response Protocols: Our customized solutions include detailed emergency response protocols that are tailored to your property layout and potential threats. This ensures that our team knows exactly how to respond effectively in critical situations.
